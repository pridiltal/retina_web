### The OSEMN framework

-  The OSEMN framework is comprised of 5 major steps that help us to focus and prioritize the right data science tasks at different stages:   1. **O**btaining Data
  2. **S**crubbing Data
  3. **E**xploring Data
  4. **M**odelling Data 
  5. **I**nterpretation of Data.
